Terrestrial & Marine Ecology

Plant Ecology Lab

Investigating ecological, functional, and physiological strategies of autotrophs in terrestrial and marine ecosystems facing global change. We integrate field ecology, plant physiology, and environmental science to build sustainable futures on Earth and beyond.

Mission & Vision

English. The Plant Ecology Lab investigates ecological, functional, and physiological strategies of autotrophic organisms under environmental change. We reveal how plants respond to multiple stressors—from pollution to extreme and space-related conditions—integrating ecological, physiological, and environmental approaches.

Italiano. Il laboratorio studia le strategie ecologiche, funzionali e fisiologiche degli organismi autotrofi in ecosistemi terrestri e marini soggetti a cambiamento, analizzando le risposte a molteplici stress naturali e antropici, fino agli ambienti estremi e spaziali.

Main research areas

Ecological strategies under change

Terrestrial and marine autotroph responses to combined natural and anthropogenic pressures.

Functional & structural adaptations

Morphological, anatomical, and physiological traits of plants thriving in natural and polluted environments.

Photosynthesis in extreme environments

Photosynthetic efficiency, photoprotection mechanisms, and stress tolerance—including space missions.

Plant–soil relationships

Interactions that enhance sustainable primary production through ecological solutions.
